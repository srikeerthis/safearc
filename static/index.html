Welcome to SafeArc

An AI-powered sorting engine that plans every move before the robot arm touches anything.

Here's how it works — 4 simple steps
📷
Point your camera at the workspace
Aim at a table with 3–10 objects in view. Works best in good lighting. You can also upload a photo instead.
🔍
Click "Scan workspace"
The AI looks at your scene, finds every object, and draws a coloured box around each one. Takes about 3–5 seconds.
🗂️
Click "Generate plan"
A second AI figures out the best order to sort the objects — grouping similar things together and avoiding any people nearby.
🤖
Click "Execute in sim"
Watch a 3D robot arm carry out the full sorting plan — a safe digital preview before anything moves in the real world.
💡 The red shaded areas on screen are no-go zones — the robot will never move into them. If a person is detected near an object, that step is safely skipped.
